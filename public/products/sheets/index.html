An RPG character manager. Coming soon.


Features

Multiple Game Systems
Planned support for the following game systems:

  • All editions of Dungeons and Dragons
  • Pathfinder
  • Dragon Age
  • Shadowrun 4th and 5th Edition.
  • FATE Core
  • The Dresden Files
  • The Mistborn Adventure Game
  • Call of Cthulu
  • d6
  • d20
  • Gamma World
  • Mutants and Masterminds
  • World of Darkness
  • Savage Worlds
  • Star Wars: Edge of the Empire
  • Star Wars: Saga Edition

Import
Supports importing Dungeons and Dragons™ 4th edition characters from the iPlay4e web application and DDI Character Builder files.

Store
Load and save your characters from and to Dropbox or App.net File Storage.

Log and Share
Keep a log of your adventure and share events on App.net, Twitter, Facebook, or Google+.
